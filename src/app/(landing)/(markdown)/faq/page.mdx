# FAQ

## Getting Started

**Q: How do I create an account on Tournaments.com?**  
A: To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage and follow the prompts to enter your information. You’ll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

**Q: Is there an age requirement to join?**  
A: Yes, you must be at least 13 years old to create an account. If you are between 13 and 18, you need parental consent.

## Account Verification

**Q: How do I verify my account?**  
A: After signing up, you’ll receive an email with a verification link. Click the link to confirm your account. If you don’t receive it, check your spam folder or request a new link.

**Q: Why do I need to verify my account?**  
A: Account verification helps ensure the integrity and security of our community, preventing fraudulent activity and ensuring fair play.

## Participating in Tournaments

**Q: How do I join a tournament?**  
A: Browse the tournaments listed on our website, select one you want to enter, and follow the instructions to register. Make sure to check the tournament rules before joining!

**Q: Can I participate with friends?**  
A: Absolutely! You can invite friends to join you in team tournaments or play alongside each other in individual competitions.

## Payments and Withdrawals

**Q: How do I make payments for tournament entries?**  
A: Payments can be made securely through our platform using various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and digital wallets.

**Q: How do I withdraw my winnings?**  
A: To withdraw your winnings, go to your account settings, navigate to the "Withdrawals" section, and follow the instructions. Withdrawals typically take 5-10 business days to process.

## Technical Support

**Q: I’m experiencing technical issues. What should I do?**  
A: If you encounter any technical difficulties, please visit our [Support page](/contact) or contact our support team at support@tournaments.com for assistance.

**Q: What are the system requirements for participating in tournaments?**  
A: While requirements may vary by game, ensure your device meets the minimum specifications for the game you’re playing. A stable internet connection is also essential.

## Security and Privacy

**Q: How is my personal information protected?**  
A: We take your privacy seriously. Our platform uses industry-standard encryption to protect your data. For more details, please refer to our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

**Q: What should I do if I suspect my account has been compromised?**  
A: If you believe your account has been hacked, change your password immediately and contact our support team for further assistance.

## Responsible Gaming

**Q: How does Tournaments.com promote responsible gaming?**  
A: We encourage players to set limits and play within their means. Resources and support for responsible gaming are available on our website.

**Q: What should I do if I feel I have a gaming problem?**  
A: If you or someone you know is struggling with gaming addiction, we recommend seeking help from a professional or contacting support organizations dedicated to gaming issues.
